# Telco Customer Churn Analysis — Business Recommendations

## 1. Focus on Early Customer Retention

Since nearly **45–50% of new customers churn within the first year**, improving the early customer experience is critical.

Actions:

* Introduce onboarding programs for new customers
* Provide early-stage discounts or incentives
* Increase engagement in the first 3–6 months

---

## 2. Encourage Long-Term Contracts

Customers on long-term contracts have churn rates as low as **10–15%**, compared to **40%+ for month-to-month users**.

Actions:

* Offer discounts for annual or two-year plans
* Provide incentives to upgrade from monthly to long-term contracts
* Bundle services for committed users

---

## 3. Revisit Pricing Strategy for High-Value Customers

Customers paying higher monthly charges (>70) have churn rates up to **35–40%**.

Actions:

* Re-evaluate pricing tiers
* Offer bundled plans to increase perceived value
* Provide targeted offers to high-paying customers at risk

---

## 4. Improve Experience for Fiber Optic Users

Fiber optic customers show higher churn despite being premium users.

Actions:

* Investigate service quality and reliability
* Improve customer support for premium users
* Offer loyalty benefits to retain high-value customers

---

## 5. Promote Value-Added Services

Customers using tech support and security services churn at nearly **half the rate** of others.

Actions:

* Offer free trials for support services
* Bundle these services with existing plans
* Increase awareness of their benefits

---

## 6. Encourage Automated Payment Methods

Customers using electronic check show higher churn compared to those using automatic payment methods.

Actions:

* Incentivize auto-pay enrollment
* Offer small discounts for automated billing
* Simplify the payment experience

---

## 7. Target High-Risk Segments Proactively

Customers who are:

* New (≤12 months)
* On month-to-month contracts
* Paying higher charges

form the highest-risk group for churn.

Actions:

* Build targeted retention campaigns
* Use personalized communication and offers
* Prioritize these customers for intervention
